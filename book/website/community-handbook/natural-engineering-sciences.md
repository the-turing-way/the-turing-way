# Showcasing Natural and Engineering Sciences (NES) in _The Turing Way_

_The Turing Way_ provides an inclusive space for researchers and practitioners from across disciplines to collaborate on reproducible, ethical, and open research.  
This page highlights how members of the **Natural and Engineering Sciences (NES)** community can engage with and contribute to _The Turing Way_.

## Why NES and _The Turing Way_?

The Natural and Engineering Sciences (NES) are foundational to many data-driven and computational projects.  
By connecting NES researchers and engineers to _The Turing Way_ community, we can:
- Share reproducible research practices across scientific domains.  
- Learn from existing open science tools and community structures.  
- Build a bridge between national NES activities (for example, those in the Netherlands) and international open science movements.
